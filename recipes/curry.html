Potato Curry Recipe

Description

This Potato curry is a simple flavorful curry made with sharp onions, tangy tomatoes, fresh herbs and fragrant spices. This is a quick and tasty one pot aloo curry. The recipe is also gluten-free and vegan

Potato is known as “Aloo” or “Alu” in Hindi. Thus the name of any curry or gravy made only with potatoes is called Aloo Curry or Aloo Gravy in Hindi

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped garlic
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions.
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • spice powders
  • potatoes

Steps

  1. Heat 3 tablespoons oil in a pan
  2. Add Garlic
  3. Next ,add chopped onions at medium low heat
  4. Then add chopped tomatoes
  5. Mix very well
  6. Cook at low heat until tomatoes softens
  7. then add 1/ 2 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon red chilli powder, 1/ 2 teaspoon coriander powder, 1/ 2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  8. Mix spice powders very well
  9. Then add 4 chopped potatoes and 2 cup water
  10. Wait 7 to 8 minutes, now curry will be ready to serve
